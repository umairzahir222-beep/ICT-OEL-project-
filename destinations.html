Top 6 Must-Visit Places

1. Hunza Valley

Known for snow mountains, peaceful lifestyle & friendly locals.


2. Swat Valley

Green valleys, blue rivers & snowfall attract thousands of tourists yearly.


3. Skardu & K2 Basecamp

Home to world’s 2nd highest mountain K2.


4. Badshahi Mosque, Lahore

A Mughal masterpiece symbolizing Islamic architecture.


5. Karachi Beach - Clifton

Seaside fun, camel rides & seafood market.


6. Fairy Meadows

Fairy Meadows is considered one of the most beautiful camping sites in the world.
