Welcome to Pakistan

Pakistan is a country located in South Asia, enriched with Mountains, Lakes, Forests, Deserts, Historical Monuments, Multi-cultural traditions and warm hospitality. It is one of the most diverse tourism spots in the world, offering everything from adventure tourism in Gilgit-Baltistan to historical sightseeing in Lahore, sandy beaches in Karachi and ancient archaeological sites like Mohenjo-daro. The beauty of Pakistan lies not only in nature but also in people who welcome guests with love and respect.

Why Visiting Pakistan?

  • World’s highest mountain ranges including Karakoram & Himalayas
  • Rich Mughal architecture and ancient heritage
  • Delicious foods like Biryani, Namkeen Gosht, Chapli Kabab
  • Friendly culture and warm-hearted locals
  • Affordable tourism compared to other countries

Pakistan Aerial View

Pakistan has been proudly recognized as one of the best tourist countries in the world. International travel vloggers like Eva Zu Beck highlighted Pakistan as a safe and beautiful destination which boosted tourism worldwide.

Historical Background

The land of Pakistan has deep historical roots of Indus Valley Civilization (2500 BC). Muslim rule, Mughal Empire and British period shaped the culture significantly. Many UNESCO heritage sites exist here like:

  1. Mohenjo-daro – One of the oldest civilizations
  2. Takht-i-Bahi Monastery – Buddhist remains
  3. Lahore Fort & Shalimar Gardens
  4. Makli Necropolis – One of the world’s largest graveyards
