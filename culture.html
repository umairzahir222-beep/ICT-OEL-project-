Languages & Music

Pakistan has more than 70 spoken languages. Urdu is national language while Punjabi, Pashto, Sindhi, Balochi and Saraiki are major local languages. Qawwali, Sufi music and traditional instruments like Rubab & Dhol are part of cultural events.

Clothing

Traditional dress is Shalwar Kameez. Cultural dresses vary by provinces.

Traditional Food

Pakistan has one of the most delicious cuisines in the world.
